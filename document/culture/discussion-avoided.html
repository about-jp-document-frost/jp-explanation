1. Harmony (和) and conflict avoidance

  • Priority: group harmony over personal opinion.
  • Risk: open disagreement can seem disruptive.
  • Result: people often stay quiet to avoid tension.

2. Fear of making mistakes

  • Cause 1: school rewards correct answers, not experimentation.
  • Cause 2: debate training and rebuttal practice are very limited in schools.
  • Mismatch: real discussion requires trial and error.
  • Result: mistakes feel risky, so people stay silent.
  • Result: people avoid discussion.
  • Reinforcement: conformity, hierarchy, and "reading the air" keep this pattern in place.

4. Why Changing Cultural Communication Styles Is Extremely Difficult

Communication styles are deeply cultural. They cannot be changed quickly by policy or criticism.

  • Habits are built over long periods.
  • Schools and workplaces reinforce them.
  • Rapid change is hard in any culture.
