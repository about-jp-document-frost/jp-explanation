Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Q. Do trains usually run on time?
💬

A. Most train lines are punctual.

Delays can still happen during rush hour, bad weather, or heavy snow in regions such as Hokuriku.

⚠️ Note: In some cases, delays may last from about 30 minutes to over an hour.

❓ Q. Is cash still necessary?
📌

A. Cashless payment is growing, especially in Tokyo, Osaka, and other major cities.

However, some shops in these cities are still cash-only.

⚠️ Note: In most rural areas, cash is essential, so having cash on hand is necessary.

❓ Q. How should I take out the garbage?
📌

A. Garbage rules and collection days differ by area.

Always check your local municipality or apartment guidance.

⚠️ Note: Some buildings may penalize late-night disposal, so timing matters.

❓ Q. What are the emergency phone numbers?
🚨

A. Police: 110.

Fire and ambulance: 119.

⚠️ Note: Save these numbers in your phone in advance.
