Entertainment and Language Accessibility in Japan

This page summarizes why many people in Japan can enjoy global entertainment without needing strong English skills.

Summary

Almost everything from foreign countries are translated into Japanese so many people don't need to understand English to enjoy entertainment, such as movies, music, and video games.

Key Point

  • Localization in Japanese reduces the need for English in everyday entertainment consumption.
